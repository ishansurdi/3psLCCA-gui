Road Rise and Fall

As per Clause 6.5 of IRC-SP:30 2019 Manual on Economy Evaluation of Highway projects in India:

The vertical profile of the road is measured in terms of meters of Rise and Fall (RF) per kilometer. The horizontal curvature is measured by the average degree of curvature (CV) per kilometer. Typical values of RF are presented in Table 4 of IRC-SP:30 2019. RF and CV are highly correlated, and it is generally sufficient to consider one of the two in the analysis.
