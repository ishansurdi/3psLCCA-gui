Wind Speed

As per Clause 209.2 of IRC:6-2017 (Standard Specifications and Code of Practice for Road Bridges, Section: II Loads and Load Combinations, Seventh Revision):

The wind speed at the location of the bridge shall be based on a basic wind speed map as shown in Fig. 10. The intensity of wind force shall be based on hourly mean wind speed and pressure as shown in Table 12. The hourly mean wind speed and pressure values given in Table 12 correspond to a basic wind speed of 33 m/s, return period of 100 years, for bridges situated in plain terrain and terrain with obstructions, with a flat topography. The hourly mean wind pressure shall be appropriately modified depending on the location of the bridge for other basic wind speed as shown in Fig. 10 and used for design (see notes below Table 12).
