# Footpath

As per Clause 104.3.6 of IRC:5 2015 *(Standard Specification and Code of Practice for Road Bridges, Section-I: General Features of Design, Eighth Revision)*:

When a footpath is provided, its clear width shall not be less than 1.5 m. For urban and populated areas having large concentrations of pedestrian traffic, the width of the footpath shall be suitably increased.
