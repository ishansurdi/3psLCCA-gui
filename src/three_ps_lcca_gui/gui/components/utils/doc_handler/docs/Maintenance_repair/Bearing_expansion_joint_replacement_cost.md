# Bearing and Expansion Joints Replacement Costs

The replacement cost of bearing and expansion joints is defined as the total economic expenditure required to remove a degraded component and install a new one, inclusive of both direct agency costs and indirect social costs.

$$RC_{BE} = PWF \times PSCR$$

where:
- **$RC_{BE}$**: bearing and expansion joints replacement cost
- **$PWF$**: present worth factor
- **$PSCR$**: percentage of superstructure construction cost factor for replacement of bearing and expansion joints
