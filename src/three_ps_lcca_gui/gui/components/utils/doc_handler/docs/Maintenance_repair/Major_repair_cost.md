# Major Repair Cost

Major repair cost is the expenditure incurred to restore the structural capacity, safety, or serviceability of a bridge when significant deterioration or damage has occurred. It includes costs related to structural strengthening, rehabilitation of damaged components, specialized labour, equipment, and materials required to reinstate the bridge to its intended performance level.

$$MRC = PWF \times PICR$$

where:
- **$MRC$**: major repair cost
- **$PWF$**: present worth factor
- **$PICR$**: percentage of initial construction cost for major repair cost
