# Workzone Multiplier

Workzone multiplier is a factor used to adjust calculated traffic delay and vehicle operating cost to reflect the real-world impact of construction, maintenance, or rehabilitation activities on road users.

This multiplier typically accounts for increased congestion, safety risks, and traveler delays that occur when capacity is reduced. It ensures that LCCA fully accounts for the total social cost of construction, shifting focus from initial construction costs alone to include the long-term cost of ownership and operation.
